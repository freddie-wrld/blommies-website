Meet Renata & Adele

Blommies isn't just a flower shop—it's a passion project built on all-round happy vibes. Nestled inside our iconic teeny tiny church right here in Bothaville, we believe that flowers have a unique language of comfort, celebration, and love.

Whether you’re stopping by 29 Botha Street to grab a quick bunch of roses, planning your dream wedding layout, or wanting to surprise someone with a beautifully curated gift box, we pour our hearts into every single stem. Come pop in, smell the lilies, and let's create something beautiful together.
